## Acknowledgments

The methodological approach was influenced by:

- **Tim Meyer**, *Strom* — for journalistic clarity on the German energy debate
- **Christian Stöcker**, *Männer, die die Welt verbrennen* — for lobby-structure analysis
- **Hans-Werner Sinn** — for the discipline of asking unwelcome questions
- **Volker Quaschning** — for sustained quantitative advocacy of EE pathways
- **Lion Hirth** (Hertie School) — for early thinking on storage requirements
- **Christian von Hirschhausen** (TU Berlin/DIW) — for nuclear cost realism

The institutional studies that anchor the model defaults are credited in
`docs/SOURCES.md` with full citations.

The model's open-source approach was inspired by the principle that
**robust analytical claims should be reproducible by anyone, not just by
their author.** This is an explicit response to the prevailing style of
German energy-policy publication, where computational details are usually
opaque or proprietary.
